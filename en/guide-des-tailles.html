Size guide

Everything you need to know to choose the perfect size — measurements, advice by body type and the specifics of each model.

How to measure yourself

  • ① Chest — under the arms, across the fullest part
  • ② Waist — at the narrowest point of your torso
  • ③ Hips — at the widest point
  • ④ Shoulder length — from nape to shoulder tip

Our advice

Measure yourself in underwear, with the tape measure lying flat against your body without pulling. If you are between two sizes, we recommend sizing up — the generous drape of our coats is one of their greatest qualities.

Practical tips

📏

Between two sizes

For our short coats (Yūn Court), choose the larger size for a more enveloping feel. For long coats, stay with your usual size.

🧥

Layering

If you often wear your coat over thick jumpers or jackets, size up. Our coats are designed to be worn over thin to medium-weight clothing.

📐

Tall stature

For those over 175 cm, the indicated lengths (~90 and ~120 cm) fall at mid-thigh and calf respectively.

↩

No risk

Still unsure? Order — you have 30 days to return your coat if the size doesn't suit you. Free returns, no questions asked.
